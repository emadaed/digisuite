2. Predictive Stock Replenishment & Inventory Control Tool

The Challenge: Frequent **stock-outs** and inconsistent, unknown **lead times** led to missed sales and customer delays.

The Solution: A data-powered tool built with **Python** (CLI + GUI). Logic calculates the **Reorder Point (ROP)** by factoring in Lead Time and necessary **Buffer Stock** based on demand variability.

Tech Stack:

Measurable Results:

  • Provided a **Smart System** for **Peace of Mind** regarding inventory.
  • Established clear, **data-backed lead times** and reduced stock-outs.
